What is Where to park?

Where to park is a website that serves to show car owners where are the available URA parking spots to better plan their journey. On top of available carparks for both motorcyclist, lorry owners and car owners, the carpark rates are also displayed and categorised based on popular locations, giving drivers a one stop place to check out before they head off.
